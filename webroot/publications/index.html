Publications related to MMT

Metrics monitoring tool is part of Pekka Mäkiaho's PhD thesis which will be published during the year 2019. During the developing process there have been published seven papers.

Risks management in software development capstone projects (September 2018).

Authors: Pekka Mäkiaho, Timo.T.Poranen

Presented in CompSysTech'18, Ruse, Bulgaria at 14th of September.

Construction of a project monitoring application iteratively and incrementally (August, 2018)

Authors: Pekka Mäkiaho, Katriina Löytty, Timo.T.Poranen

Presented at Constructionism 2018, Vilnius, Lithuania at 23rd of August.

Requirements Management in Students' Software Development Projects (June, 2017)

Authors: Pekka Mäkiaho, Timo.T.Poranen, Zheying Zhang

Publication: Mäkiaho, P. and Poranen, T and Zhang, Z..: Requirements Management in Students' Software Development Projects, Proceedings of CompSysTech 2017, pp. 203-210.

MMT - A Tool for Observing Metrics in Software Projects (2017) - Presentation of a previous version at eLearning conferemce, Bratislava 2016

Authors: Pekka Mäkiaho, Timo.T.Poranen, Katriina Vartiainen

Publication: Mäkiaho, P. and Poranen, T and Vartiainen K.: MMT - A Tool for Observing Metrics in Software Projects, International Journal of Human Capital and Information Technology Professionals 8(4), 2017, pp. 27-37.

Software Metrics in Students’ Software Development Projects (2015)

Authors: Pekka Mäkiaho, Timo.T.Poranen, Ari Seppi

Publication: Mäkiaho, P. and Poranen, T. and Seppi, A.: Software Metrics in Students’ Software Development Projects, Proceedings of CompSysTech 2015, 8 pages.

Version Control Usage in Students’ Software Development Projects (2014)

Authors: Pekka Mäkiaho, Timo.T.Poranen, Ari Seppi

Publication:Mäkiaho, P. and Poranen, T. and Seppi, A.: Version Control Usage in Students’ Software Development Projects, Proceedings of CompSysTech 2014, 8 pages.

Tool usage in students' software projects Projects (2012)

Authors: Pekka Mäkiaho, Timo.T.Poranen

Publication:Mäkiaho, P. and Poranen, T.: Tool usage in students' software projects, 2012, Proceedings of INSPIRE, 2012, 63-76.
